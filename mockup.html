The one-paragraph pitch: Serbizyu is the trust infrastructure for the Philippine local service economy. Buyers book verified neighbors — or post what they need and let servicers bid. Every peso sits in escrow until the work is confirmed. Every job follows a visible step-by-step contract. Every screen below is clickable — open one and keep tapping.

🧭 How the system works — two loops, one engine

Every transaction, whichever direction it starts, ends up in the same escrow + tracking + review pipeline.

servicer lists an offer → buyer books it directly
Home / Search→Offer page→Pay into escrow→Live tracking→Confirm & release→Review & rebook

The catalog path: tricycle rides, tutors, plumbers — fixed prices, bookable now. This is what the Home feed leads with.
