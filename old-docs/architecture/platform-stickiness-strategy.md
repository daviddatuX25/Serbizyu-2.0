
## Platform Stickiness Strategy

*Saved from 2026-07-19 design session.*

The platform competes against "I'll just use this to surface my service then take deals offline."
Strategy: make being ON the platform MORE valuable than the cash bypass.

| Mechanism | Why They Stay |
|---|---|
| **Reviews accumulate** | Offline deal = no review. After 50 reviews, your profile is an asset. Walk away = lose it. |
| **Verification tiers** | Tier 3+ unlocks featured placement, lower dispute holds, higher booking limits. Tiers need platform history. |
| **Dispute protection** | Cash-only deals have zero recourse. Buyer pays → you don't show up → you get reported. Platform escrow protects BOTH sides. |
| **Repeat customer flow** | Buyer books you again in 2 taps from order history. Offline = find number, text, negotiate from scratch. |
| **Agent commission** | Agent only earns on platform-tracked deals. They'll push owners to keep it on-platform. |
| **SMS habit loop** | Owner gets weekly "you earned ₱920" — they SEE the value. If deals go offline, the SMS stops. |
| **Cash acceptance** | We don't fight cash — we record it, charge 12%, and make the platform experience so good that digital (8%) is preferred. |

The platform doesn't fight cash — it makes being ON the platform so much better that cash is the fallback, not the default.
